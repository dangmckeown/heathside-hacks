Training

London Heathside provides a choice of training venues throughout North London, including the Finsbury Park track & field facility, the YMCA Fitness Centre in Crouch End, the Royal Free Recreation Club in Hampstead, Trent Park in Cockfosters and Sunday runs from Highgate Tube Station. Each location has a different focus for training, with something to suit everyone’s tastes and needs.

 

This is the typical weekly training schedule for senior athletes: 

 

Tuesdays


Hornsey YMCA in Crouch End, N8 8SG, 7:30pm

From September to April this session involves steady runs on pavement covering 6-9 miles. From the end of April to August the sessions are coached interval training for all levels around Highgate Woods, always with the option of a steady run. We assign members to look after new starters. This is a great introduction to our running club for those who have been training on their own and are capable of 30-40 minutes of steady running or jogging.  Contact Barbara on barbarat@londonheathside.org.uk

 

Finsbury Park Track, N4

Interval-based track sessions led by qualified coaches for middle distance and endurance athletes from 7:00pm onwards.  Contact Jerry on chairman@londonheathside.org.uk for details and more information.

 

For sprinters and jumpers, strength and conditioning training is held in our dedicated gym from 6pm onwards and a range of speed development track training from 7:30pm onwards. Contact Sam on slsb.brown@gmail.com for details and more information

 

Showers and lockers available at the track & field facility.

 

 

Wednesdays


Royal Free Recreation Club in Hampstead, NW3, 7:00pm 

A steady run or interval session on the road in the autumn/winter, with trail runs over Hampstead Heath in the summer months.

 

 

Thursdays


Hornsey YMCA, Crouch End, 7:30pm 

Option of a hill session or a steady run. The hill sessions run from mid-Sept to mid-Feb and take place around Denton Rd/Uplands Rd etc in the Crouch End area. Details are announced each week.

 

Finsbury Park Track, N4

Interval-based track sessions led by qualified coaches for middle distance and endurance athletes from 7:00pm onwards.  Contact Jerry on chairman@londonheathside.org.uk for details and more information.

 

For sprinters and jumpers, strength and conditioning training is held in our dedicated gym from 6pm onwards and a range of speed development track training from 7:30pm onwards. Contact Sam on slsb.brown@gmail.com for details and more information

 

Showers and lockers are available.

 

 

Saturdays

10:15am Trent Park, Cockfosters. Cross-country training/intervals except on winter race days. Contact Jerry on jerryodlin@mac.com

 

 

Sundays

Highgate Tube Station for long runs (meet at corner of Archway Rd and Shepherds Hill):

9am long run 15+ miles at varied pace but probably sub-7:30min/mile on average
10am steady run 8-10 miles
11am 6-8 miles easy over Hampstead Heath (8:30 to 10mins/mile)


Please note: these training runs are not formally coached and we recommend coming to another of the weekly training groups first to get to know the club


Finsbury Park Track, N4

For sprinters and jumpers, strength and conditioning training in our dedicated gym from 10.00am onwards and a range of speed development track training from 11:00am onwards led by a qualified sprint coaches. Contact Sam on slsb.brown@gmail.com for details and more information
